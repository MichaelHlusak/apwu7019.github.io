The Data Feedback is the Local's newsletter. It is published once every two months. The Local is currently evaluating the possibility of offering the PDF version of the newsletter online.

Submission Policy: All members are encouraged to submit items and articles for publication. Deadline for all submissions is by the 1st of the month. Opinions expressed are not necessarily those of the editor, the Twin Cities Postal Data Center Local, or the APWU. Articles may be submitted here or sent to: tcapwu7019editor@gmail.com. No hard copy requiring retyping will be accepted. Please contact the editor if you have another format.
